About Us

ROMBLON TRANSPORT COOPERATIVE (RTC) was formed in compliance with the PUV modernization program. The existing local transport operators and van owners joined force to form the cooperative complying with the standards set by law.

RTC organized the association of Tourist, UV Express, Jeepney and Rental transport services in the Province of Romblon to provide quality transport services.

RTC aims to address transport problem and support the development and effort of the government to modernize transport system and ultimately bring about economic development and progress.
